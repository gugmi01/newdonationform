Support Breakthrough with a Donation by Credit or Debit Card

Thank you for supporting us this benefit season. The majority of the support to Breakthrough Urban Ministries comes from individuals just like you. It is your gifts that enables us to continue doing life-changing work throughout the year. All funds will go towards our mission to break the cycle of poverty by connecting youth and families to a network of opportunities and empowering urban communities to achieve self-sufficiency.

To make a gift by mail, send your check made out to Breakthrough Urban Ministries to P.O. Box 47200, Chicago, IL 60647. If you have any questions about making a donation, please call 773.722.1144.
